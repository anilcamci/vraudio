VIRTUAL REALITY AUDIO

A GUIDE TO IMMERSIVE SOUND IN VIRTUAL EXPERIENCES

by Anıl Çamcı and Jason Corey

Welcome to the companion website for Virtual Reality Audio. Here, you will find downloadable examples and projects that accompany selected chapters of the book. We will update these examples as their software platforms are updated with new features or bug fixes. We will also add new examples that demonstrate other concepts and techniques from the book, as well as new developments in the field of VR audio. If you encounter any issues with downloading these materials, please let us know at info@vraudio.org.

Chapter 3

Inviso Binaural Test

This Inviso project includes a virtual auditory scene with two sounds: one that circles around the user and another one that moves up and down. The head model in the middle represents the vantage point from which we hear this scene. The HRTFs used to spatialize the sound are generic so listeners may experience front-back or up-down confusions or other errors in sound source localization.

Chapter 5

Case Study: Percussion Ensemble VR Concert

In this case study, we offer the production materials for a VR concert featuring the University of Michigan's Percussion Ensemble. The materials include a 3D 360° video file, first-order and second-order Ambisonic recordings, spot and binaural microphone recordings, as well as the Reaper project file.

In this case study, we present a VR game built in Unity. Named "The Fountain," this game features fully procedural audio with libPd and spatialization with Steam Audio. The project materials include the Unity project, and all the associated audio and visual assets.
